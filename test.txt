# Referral Program Components and Best Practices

## Email Communications

### Referrer Auto Invite Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Real-time after user transaction or on-demand via Buyapowa's sign-ups API
**Purpose:** Send invites to specific customer subsets

**Best Practices:**
- Avoid confusing jargon - keep invitations clear, simple, and attractive
- Message the proposition with a referral cap in mind for powerful 'up to' messaging (e.g., 'Earn up to £500 in rewards' vs 'Get a £10 reward')
- Use multiple referral language - 'referring friends' not 'a friend' to clarify multiple rewards opportunity
- Include email address or webpage for customer help in fine print (required in all emails)

---

### Referrer Invite Nurture Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Purpose:** Re-target users who did not respond to initial Referrer Auto Invite email
**Behavior:** Optional repeats end once user interacts

**Best Practices:**
- Write entirely different body content or repeat initial invite approach
- Use different hero image, subject line, and preview line to avoid repetitive feeling
- Ensure recipient doesn't feel like they're receiving identical emails repeatedly

---

### Referrer Sign Up Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Sent after users enter details on Referrer Sign Up Page

**Best Practices:**
- Message the proposition with referral cap for powerful 'up to' messaging
- Use multiple referral language - 'referring friends' not 'a friend'
- Clarify users can refer multiple friends for multiple rewards, not one-and-done activity

---

### Referrer Nurture Email Sequence

**Component Type:** Email sequence
**Purpose:** Keep referrers engaged and continually sharing their unique link
**Timing:** Sent at nominated intervals after initial sign up

**Best Practices:**
- Highlight different rewards or incentives each month with multiple variations
- Include USPs that referrers can use to entice friends
- Create different images for each email and vary copy to avoid repetition
- Work with Buyapowa's Client Success and Content teams for sequence optimization

---

### Friend Instructions Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Sent after friend submits details on Friend Registration Page
**Purpose:** Encourage transaction and provide user journey route back

**Best Practices:**
- Reiterate considered purchase concept - offer locked in for X time period
- Remind of reward value and brand switching benefits
- Acknowledge friends unlikely to transact immediately upon receipt

---

### PPT Friend Instructions Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Sent after friend submits details on Friend Registration Page
**Purpose:** Encourage transaction and self-reporting to complete referral

**Best Practices:**
- Provide route back into referral program
- Reiterate considered purchase concept with time-locked offer
- Remind of reward value and brand benefits for when ready to transact

---

### PPT Friend Reminder Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Timing:** Sent at nominated interval after Friend Instruction Email if no self-reporting seen
**Condition:** Not sent if friend already performed required action

**Best Practices:**
- Convey stronger urgency element than Friend Instruction Email
- Position as last chance to convert friend into customer
- Emphasize urgency in subject, header, and image

---

### Friend Nurture Email Sequence

**Component Type:** Email sequence
**Purpose:** Remind registered friends about offers and available rewards
**Behavior:** Automatically stops once friend transacts
**Requirement:** Must always contain CTA to transact when ready

**Best Practices:**
- Highlight different rewards or brand USPs monthly with variations
- Recommend three different emails for optimal sequence
- Create different images and vary copy to prevent repetition
- Leverage considered purchase context to keep brand top-of-mind

---

### (P) Friend Nurture Email Sequence

**Component Type:** Email sequence  
**Purpose:** Remind registered friends about offers and rewards
**Behavior:** Stops after friend transacts and passes validation
**Requirement:** Must contain CTA to transact when ready

**Best Practices:**
- Same as Friend Nurture Email Sequence above
- Reassure friends who transacted but await validation that rewards come after confirmation

---

### Referrer Pending Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** When friend successfully transacts but referral awaits validation

**Best Practices:**
- Celebrate success while driving multiple referrals
- Remind users of total reward value for continued referring
- Set clear expectations for reward timing, especially if significant waiting period

---

### (P) Referrer Pending Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** When friend successfully signs up but hasn't transacted yet

**Best Practices:**
- Celebrate success while driving multiple referrals
- Set expectations about remaining friend steps required
- Clarify reward timing since referral not yet confirmed

---

### Friend Pending Email

**Component Type:** Email (optional)
**Trigger:** When friend successfully transacts but referral awaits validation

**Best Practices:**
- Keep friend informed of reward progress
- Set expectations for reward timing
- No action required from friend at this stage

---

### Referrer Claim Reward Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Upon confirmation of pending referral

**Best Practices:**
- Include two CTAs: one for Claim Reward page, another for referral dashboard
- Use email to drive multiple referrals from every referrer

---

### Friend Claim Reward Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** Upon confirmation of pending referral

**Best Practices:**
- Include two CTAs: one for Claim Reward page, another for referrer sign-up
- Convert friend into referrer for their own friend invitations

---

### Referrer Reward Delivery Email

**Component Type:** Email (multiple versions required)
**Trigger:** After referrer selects reward
**Requirement:** One version per reward type (excluding PayPal)
**Content:** May contain digital redemption codes or hyperlinks

**Best Practices:**
- Reference digital codes/links in subject line (e.g., "Your $50 Amazon Gift Card is inside")
- Include code usage instructions
- Include CTA linking back to referrer dashboard for repeat referrals

---

### Friend Reward Delivery Email

**Component Type:** Email (multiple versions required)
**Trigger:** After friend selects reward  
**Requirement:** One version per reward type (excluding PayPal)
**Content:** May contain digital redemption codes or hyperlinks

**Best Practices:**
- Reference digital codes/links in subject line
- Include CTA to convert friend into referrer
- Link to dashboard (with active cookie) or Referrer Sign Up Page

---

### Referrer Reward Cap Limit Reached Email

**Component Type:** Email
**Trigger:** When referrer meets program reward cap

**Best Practices:**
- Set clear expectations for when rewards can resume
- Clarify if cap is lifetime or time-based

---

## Web Pages

### Referrer Sign Up Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Purpose:** Allow users to sign up for refer-a-friend program

**Best Practices:**
- Use image and page title for dual headline messaging opportunities
- Message total 'up to' reward value and per-referral amount separately
- Use dynamic CTA language like 'Generate my link' vs dry CTAs like 'Submit'
- Support maximum of 3 steps in instructional text

---

### Referrer Dashboard Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Purpose:** Provide signed-up referrers with unique sharing links and referral tracking

**Best Practices:**
- Default sharing text should reflect friend proposition and risk-free offer
- Text represents first brand exposure to friends - must entice click-through
- Follow golden rule: would you be interested if a friend sent this?

---

### Friend Registration Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Accessed via:** Referrer's unique link clicks

**Best Practices:**
- Use 'considered purchase' language acknowledging friends may not be ready to purchase immediately
- Convince friends to 'lock in offer' by entering details with arbitrary time limit
- Don't overwhelm with details - focus on compelling image, headline, USPs, rewards, and considered purchase messaging
- Reference referrer's name for social proof and reassurance
- Primary goal: get form completion for nurturing until ready to transact

---

### Friend Post-Registration Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Purpose:** Confirm successful Friend Registration Page completion and provide next steps

**Best Practices:**
- Confirm 'offer is locked in' and no immediate transaction requirement
- Provide purchase journey route for ready users
- Use single 'shop now' CTA or highlight specific products/packages
- Absolute limit of 6 CTAs, recommend maximum 3 for simplicity

---

### Friend Self-Reporting Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Access:** Immediate after Friend Registration Page submission or via magic link in emails
**Purpose:** Submit unique identifier for referral tracking

**Best Practices:**
- Don't overwhelm with details - keep simple for nurturing purposes
- Reference referrer's name for social proof and reassurance
- Primary goal: form completion for continued nurturing

---

### Claim Reward Pages

**Component Type:** Web pages
**Purpose:** Allow referrers and friends to claim rewards via email links

**Best Practices:**
- Keep reward titles and descriptions brief
- Support up to 6 reward choices, recommend maximum 3 for tight messaging
- Manage delivery time expectations for physical rewards

---

### FAQ Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Access:** Via 'FAQ' links on Claim Reward pages and other program interfaces

**Best Practices:**
- Include comprehensive questions and answers covering program mechanics
- Cover rewards on offer and relevant business rules
- Don't just repeat examples - customize for your specific program

---

### Terms & Conditions Page

**Component Type:** Web page
**Access:** Via terms and conditions links on program pages and emails
**Options:** Host on your website (provide URL) or Buyapowa hosts (provide full copy)

---

## Interactive Features

### Post-Purchase Referrer Popover

**Component Type:** Interactive feature
**Trigger:** Customer purchase completion
**Purpose:** Alert and educate about refer-a-friend program opportunities

**Best Practices:**
- Surface at optimal moment when customer demonstrated purchase willingness
- Capture high-potential traffic at funnel top
- Keep simple: headline, brief description, CTA, and fine print
- Second panel shows unique sharing link with easy copy/share functionality for desktop and mobile
- Enroll customers immediately while contact information is fresh
